ACM ROBOTATHON

about

Come join us at UHD's third annual Robotathon! All majors and skill levels are encouraged to participate in this event. This year we will have two tracks, one for BEGINNERS and one for ADVANCED. Once you choose a track, you may form groups of 4 MAX and start the competition!


When

Friday March 31st, 2023!

From 08:30am - 4pm


where

Join us in the Science and Technology Building, Fondren Room. This room is located on the first floor of the new Science & Technology building at UHD!.
